Our Story

A Sanctuary Amidst the Mist

Located in the heart of Deniyaya, Beverly Villa is more than just a stay—it's an immersion into the natural beauty of Sri Lanka's southern highlands.

Surrounded by a private tea estate and overlooking the emerald canopy of the Sinharaja Rainforest, our bungalow offers the perfect blend of colonial charm and modern comfort.

  • 🌿 100% Privacy & Seclusion
  • 🏔️ Breathtaking Mountain Views
