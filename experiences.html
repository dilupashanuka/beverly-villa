Things To Do

Sinharaja Rainforest

Just 5.8 km from the villa, Sinharaja is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a biodiversity hotspot. Perfect for bird watching and nature treks.

Patna Sliding Rock

A natural water slide created by nature over thousands of years. A favorite spot for both locals and travelers for a refreshing dip.

Magical Waterfalls

Explore Sathmala Ella, Kekuna Ella, and many other hidden waterfalls in the lush Deniyaya jungle, all within easy reach.

Tea Estate Walks

Experience the life of a tea plantation. Walk through our own green rolling hills and learn about the journey of Ceylon tea.
